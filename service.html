Hotels Booking


From the moment you arrive, our hotel services will have you feeling like royalty with exclusive amenities and personalized attention!

Suite

Our hotel suite class is a luxurious accommodation option that offers an elevated level of comfort and convenience. These suites are typically more spacious than standard hotel rooms, featuring separate living areas and bedrooms. They may also include additional amenities such as a kitchenette or full kitchen, a dining area, and a private balcony or terrace with scenic views.

Deluxe

Our hotel's Deluxe class is an ideal choice for guests seeking a comfortable and upscale accommodation experience. These rooms are typically more spacious than standard hotel rooms and are thoughtfully designed with a focus on guest comfort and convenience. Our Deluxe rooms feature modern decor, plush bedding, and state-of-the-art amenities such as a flat-screen TV, complimentary high-speed internet, and a work desk

Cottage

Our hotel's cottage services offer guests a unique and charming accommodation option, providing a peaceful and relaxing escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Our cottages are thoughtfully designed to offer guests all the comforts of home, with fully equipped kitchens, private bathrooms, and comfortable sleeping areas.

Home-Stay

Our hotel's self-cook homestay service is an excellent option for guests looking for a more independent and flexible accommodation experience. Guests who choose our self-cook homestay service have access to fully equipped kitchen facilities, allowing them to prepare their meals according to their preferences and schedules.
